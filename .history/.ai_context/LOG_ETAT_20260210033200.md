## 🚀 Major Functional Modules

### 1. 🎯 Enterprise Accounting Hub (STOCK A - Official)
* **Commercial Pipeline:**
    * **Quotations (Devis):** Full A4 generation including "Validity Date" and specific "Bon pour accord" signature zone.
    * **Conversion Logic:** One-click transformation of **Devis → Invoice**, instantly carrying over all line items, client data, and pricing.
* **Transactional Safety (NEW v3.9.5):**
    * **Atomic Locking:** All Sales (`InvoicesController`) and Returns now use `prisma.$transaction` to prevent partial data writes.
    * **Big Data Precision:** Replaced all floating-point math with `Prisma.Decimal` to eliminate currency rounding errors (e.g., 0.1 + 0.2).
    * **Stock Logic:** * Selling **decrements** stock.
        * Returns/Cancellations/Avoirs **increment** stock automatically.
* **Tax Compliance:**
    * **Dynamic VAT:** Full support for mixed VAT rates (10% and 20%) on Invoices and Returns.
    * **Reporting:** `LegalReportController` accurately separates collected tax by rate for DGI declarations.
    * **Output:** PDF Invoices now display the specific VAT rate per line item.

### 2. ♻️ Smart Return & Restocking Engine
* **"Legacy" to Digital Pipeline:**
    * **Ghost Item Import:** Capability to accept returns for items sold *before* the digital system.
    * **Auto-Restocking:** Returned items are **instantly created** in Stock A with a generated sequence.
* **Dual-Mode Exchange:**
    * **Atomic Swaps:** Handles the Return (Credit Note) and the New Sale (Invoice) in a single database transaction.
    * **Input Safety:** Frontend enforces strict data types to prevent "NaN" or negative values during exchanges.

### 3. 🏭 Internal Operations Engine (STOCK B - Operational)
