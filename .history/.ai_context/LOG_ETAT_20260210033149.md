## 🚀 Major Functional Modules

### 1. 🏗️ Single Source of Truth (STOCK B - NEW v4.0.0)
* **Unified Ledger Architecture:**
    * **Decommissioned:** `ClientPayment` table removed to prevent data fragmentation.
    * **Consolidated:** All financial events (Sales, Returns, Payments) are now centralized in the `StockMovement` table.
    * **Native Payments:** Integrated `PAYMENT` movement type with a native `paymentRef` column for tracking Check/Transfer numbers.
* **Financial Integrity:**
    * **Atomic Debt Logic:** Client balance is managed via `Prisma.Decimal` increments/decrements within transactions.
    * **Precise Recovery:** The system handles payments as negative amount movements, allowing a simple sum of the ledger to yield current debt.

### 2. 🟣 The "Truth Engine" (Inventory & Corrections)
* **Physical/Digital Alignment:**
    * **Inventory Dashboard:** Specialized interface for shelf-by-shelf physical counting.
    * **Auto-Correction:** Logic calculates the gap between "Theoretical" (System) and "Real" (Physical) stock, generating automatic `ADJUSTMENT` entries.
* **Audit Protocol:**
    * **Mandatory Logging:** All manual stock overrides require a reason (Theft, Damage, Correction) and are logged in the movement history.
    * **Value Tracking:** Adjustments track the financial impact (Purchase Cost * Quantity Difference).

### 3. 🎯 Enterprise Accounting Hub (STOCK A - Official)
* **Commercial Pipeline:**
    * **Quotations (Devis):** Full A4 generation including "Validity Date" and specific "Bon pour accord" signature zone.
    * **Conversion Logic:** One-click transformation of **Devis → Invoice**, instantly carrying over all line items, client data, and pricing.
* **Tax Compliance:**
    * **Dynamic VAT:** Full support for mixed VAT rates (10% and 20%) on Invoices and Returns.
    * **Output:** PDF Invoices now display the specific VAT rate per line item.

### 4. 📊 Strategic Analytics & Reporting (v4.0.0)
* **Executive Dashboard:**
    * **Aggregation:** Real-time aggregation of Silo A (Legal) and Silo B (Operational) data.
    * **KPIs:** Tracks Cash Flow (Sales + Debt Recovery), Receivables (Money Out), and Total Asset Value.
* **Reporting:**
    * **PDF Export:** Integrated `jsPDF` for instant professional PDF reports of strategic dashboard metrics.
    * **SVG Charts:** Lightweight, real-time trend visualization for monthly cash flow.

### 5. 👥 Scalable Client Management (v4.0.0)
* **Unified Profile:** Merged view of a client's Legal status (Invoices) and Internal status (Debt/Stock B movements).
* **Access Control:** 3-Session Architecture (Super Admin, Legal User, POS User) with role-based routing and permissions.
